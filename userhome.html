What is Disrobe?

Disrobe is a place to store fashion inspiration. It's a new way to organize your wardrobe. It's a smarter way to shop, and it's a digital personal stylist. Just follow the steps below. You can keep your clothes on!
1

Fill out your user profile. You can use the default lists of occasions and styles, or add your own.

2

Create an inspiration library. Upload photographs from your computer or link to ones you found online.

3

Digitize your wardrobe. Snap pictures of every coat, bottom, and bauble and store them here, where you can view your whole wardrobe at a glance.

4

After completing steps 1-3, use Disrobe to generate smart shopping lists and to get outfit ideas when that "nothing to wear" feeling strikes.
